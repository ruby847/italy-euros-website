History of Italy in the Euros Italy has been a consistent participant in the UEFA European Championship since they first qualified. With a rich history in the tournament, Italy has made a significant impact, showcasing their footballing expertise and competitive spirit over the years.

Achievements
  • 1968: Champions (Hosted and won the tournament, defeating Yugoslavia in a replay final)
  • 1980: Hosted the tournament and reached the semi-finals.
  • 2000: Reached the finals, lost to France in extra time.
  • 2012: Reached the finals, lost to Spain.
  • 2020: Champions (Won against England on penalties in the final at Wembley)
