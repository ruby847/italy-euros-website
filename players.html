Italy National Team Players

This page highlights some of the key players from the Italy national football team, focusing on their contributions to the European Championships.

Player Profiles

Gianluigi Donnarumma

Position: Goalkeeper

Gianluigi Donnarumma is one of the standout players for Italy, especially known for his performance in the 2020 European Championship, where he was named the Player of the Tournament. His incredible saves, particularly in the penalty shootout against England in the final, helped secure Italy’s victory.

Leonardo Bonucci

Position: Defender

Leonardo Bonucci is a veteran defender who has been a key player for Italy in multiple tournaments. His leadership and defensive skills were crucial in the 2020 European Championship. Bonucci also scored a pivotal goal in the final against England.

Federico Chiesa

Position: Forward

Federico Chiesa is known for his speed, agility, and ability to score important goals. In the 2020 European Championship, Chiesa’s performances were vital, particularly his goal in the semi-final against Spain, which helped Italy advance to the final.
